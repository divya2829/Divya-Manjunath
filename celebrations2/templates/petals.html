White Petals

White Petals Palace Grounds in Bangalore is a well-known venue for grand events like weddings, receptions, corporate gatherings, and exhibitions. Here are some key details:

Venue Details:

  • Location: Palace Grounds, off Jayamahal Main Road, near Mekhri Circle
  • Seating Capacity: 2,000 guests
  • Floating Capacity: 3,500 to 5,000 guests
  • Spaces Available:
    • Elegant banquet hall with chandeliers and plush seating
    • Expansive, landscaped lawn suitable for large outdoor events

Facilities & Services:

  • Parking: Space for up to 450-500 vehicles with valet parking
  • Rooms: 8 complimentary changing rooms available
  • Catering: In-house catering available; non-veg food is allowed
  • Alcohol: Allowed, but outside alcohol is not permitted
  • Decor: In-house decoration provided, external decorators allowed (starting at ₹1,75,000)
  • Music & DJ: Basic music system available; in-house DJ and external DJs permitted
  • Baarat & Late-night Events: Allowed with venue-specific terms
  • Booking & Pricing:
    • Advance Payment: 50% of the total estimated amount to confirm the booking
    • Event Timings: Typically from 3:00 PM to 2:00 AM
    • Tax: 18% on food and beverages
