White Petals

White Petals, Palace Grounds, Bangalore, is taking the concept of dream venues several notches higher. It is home to a banquet hall that is the definition of royal. With resplendent crystalchandeliers and arrant lights adorning a decorative ceiling, carpeted floor, glass walls, and plush seating the baroque hall adds a tonne of tinsel to your special day. White Petals Palace Grounds also has a sprawling lawn that is neatly fenced with verdancy, adding a serene glow to your outdoor event. Both, the hall and the lawn can accommodate thousands of guests each and thus White Petals, Bangalore, Karnataka is perfect for majestic weddings, receptions, sangeet, mehndi, and engagement ceremonies, lavish birthday and wedding anniversary parties, upscale social gatherings, expos, exhibitions, award functions, promotion parties, fundraisers, auctions, product launches, and much more.
