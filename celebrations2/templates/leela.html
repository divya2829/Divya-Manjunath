Leela Palace

Details:

The Leela Palace in Kodihalli, Bangalore, is a luxurious 5-star venue spread across 9 acres. Known for its grand architecture, lush gardens, and water features, it offers an opulent setting for weddings and events.

Venue Details:

  • Kamal & Sitara: 60–80 guests
  • Nithya: 70–150 guests
  • Diya Terrace: 100–150 guests
  • Royal Ballroom: 200–300 guests
  • Grand Ballroom: 600–900 guests
  • Total Capacity: Up to 1,500 floating guests

Catering & Food:

  • In-house catering only; outside catering not allowed
  • Vegetarian menu starts at ₹3,000 per plate
  • Non-vegetarian menu starts at ₹3,500 per plate

Music & Entertainment:

  • In-house DJ available
  • Outside DJs allowed
  • Late-night music not permitted

Pricing & Booking:

  • Advance Payment: 50% of estimated cost required
  • No separate hall rental — charges based on per plate pricing
  • Valet parking available; space for 500 vehicles
  • Accommodation: 356 rooms available; average ₹15,000 per night
