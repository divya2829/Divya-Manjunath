Lalitha Mahal Palace

Venue Details:

Located in Siddhartha Layout, Mysore, Lalitha Mahal Palace is a luxurious heritage venue perfect for grand celebrations like weddings, receptions, and corporate events. As a 4-star hotel, it features regal architecture and elegant banquet halls.

  • Guest Capacity: Up to 2,500 guests
  • Largest Hall: Seats 1,000; floating capacity of 2,000
  • Banquet Halls: 3 spacious halls with central AC, dressing rooms, and dining area
  • Catering:
    • In-house catering available
    • Veg: ₹1,000/plate
    • Non-veg: ₹1,200/plate (taxes extra)
    • External caterers allowed
  • Supported Events: Weddings, receptions, sangeet, mehendi, birthdays, corporate functions

Pricing & Additional Info:

  • Hall Booking: Free with food; per plate pricing applies
  • Poolside Party: Available for up to 100 guests
  • Late-Night Weddings: Allowed with terms
  • Advance Booking: Deposit required for confirmation
